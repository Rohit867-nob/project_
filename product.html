FDAS: Fire Detection & Alarm Systems

Overview:

  • Our Fire Alarm System (FAS) is an advanced safety feature designed for railway onboard solutions.
  • It is engineered to detect the earliest signs of fire, ensuring the safety and protection of passengers and railway assets.

Features:

  • Early Detection: Utilizes high-sensitivity smoke detectors and heat sensors to provide rapid response to fire incidents.
  • Integrated System: Seamlessly integrates with onboard systems for automatic train operation control during emergencies.
  • Robust Alarm Protocol: Features visual and auditory alarms to alert passengers and crew immediately.
  • Self-Testing Functionality: Regular automatic checks ensure system readiness and operational reliability.

Advantages:

  • Enhanced Safety: Offers peace of mind with a system designed to minimize the risk of fire-related accidents.
  • Compliance with Regulations: Meets international safety standards for fire detection in railway vehicles.
  • Minimal Maintenance: Designed for durability and low maintenance, reducing lifetime costs.
