Site Survey and Layout Design

Site Survey and layout design is an important phase of any project activity from beginning to end. The impact of good layout practices in terms of time, money, and construction efficiency increases with the size of the project. Novius team typically survey and otherwise gather information about a proposed job site. As a job progresses, they lay out the location of equipment and other features on the site, check the dimensions of the placement as they are being installed, document the completed work, and verify that all the work is done in accordance with the design plans and specifications.

Once Novius has surveyed the site and gathered information on the physical space, the general operation, product flow, operational design goals and budget, we will work together to achieve an optimal layout. We consider different types of systems based on your need and budget. We draw the design utilizing Computer-Aided Design (CAD). CAD enables us to easily develop alternative layouts for your premises and manufacturing operations in the course of the planning and design. CAD has several advantages that make it more accurate and convenient than drawing by hand and relying on standard mathematical calculations. Additionally, CAD is accurate enough to produce drawings that can be used for the installation of equipment. Once you approve a design, we will specify specifications and equipment and provide either a budget or hard quote on the materials, installation, engineering and permitting labour.

Annual Maintenance Contracts- AMC/CMC

We offer 3 types of AMC which are

VISIT BASED Under this category we provide diagnosis and maintenance of system whenever we receive a call from the client regarding any problem in systems.

REGULAR Under this category we provide diagnosis and maintenance of system whenever we receive a call from the client regarding any problem in systems plus monthly visit to the sight for checking and maintenance.

COMPREHENSIVE Under this category we provide diagnosis and maintenance of system whenever we receive a call from the client regarding any problem in systems plus Monthly visit to the sight for checking and maintenance.

Benefits of the Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC/CMC) In today’s competitive era, quality is a prime factor. Quality output from any machine depends on systems repeat performance. To get repeat performance; system maintenance is a key factor. Maintenance of systems with Computerized Controls requires special skills. Every organisation may not have specialized maintenance team to take care of all variety of systems. In order to help our customers in getting the best throughput from the system, we have introduced the concept of Annual Maintenance Contracts for the customers.
